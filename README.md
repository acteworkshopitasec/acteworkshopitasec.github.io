## Mission

The mission of ACTE is to understand and address the cybersecurity challenges and opportunities created by new and evolving technologies. ACTE aims to build a multidisciplinary community that connects researchers, practitioners, and students to explore the dynamic world of cybersecurity.

The workshop promotes collaboration, advances knowledge, and supports the development of innovative frameworks and strategies that ensure technological progress goes hand in hand with security, privacy, and trust.


## Objectives

* Explore the security implications of emerging technologies, with emphasis on Generative AI.
* Promote research on predictive and anticipatory approaches to cyber threats.
* Encourage collaboration between academic, industrial, and governmental communities.
* Identify future challenges and share best practices in threat modeling and mitigation.
* Support the development of ethical, legal, and policy frameworks for secure innovation.

---

## Participation & Audience

ACTE targets researchers, Ph.D. students, practitioners, and policy makers interested in anticipatory cybersecurity, AI safety, data governance, and related fields. We welcome contributions from diverse disciplines and encourage cross‑sector collaboration.
