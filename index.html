About ACTE 2025

The "Cyber Threats' Evolution (ACTE)" workshop will provide a multidisciplinary exploration of emerging cyber threats, focusing on innovative approaches for their prevention, counteraction, and mitigation. Aligned with the National Cybersecurity Strategy 2022-2026 from the Italian National Agency for Cybersecurity (ACN), the event will address the challenge of anticipating the evolution of cyber threats, a key priority for the Italian cybersecurity agenda. This workshop offers a valuable platform for researchers from the National Ph.D. Program in Cybersecurity to share their findings and contribute to the development of advanced solutions in the field. It also provides an opportunity for Ph.D. candidates to present their research, foster collaboration, and engage in knowledge exchange with experts in the field.
